Game creation

Mode

The game mode:

  • In capture games the players compete for the words, the first player that typed a word wins it. The round winner is the player who caught the most words before the round expires.
  • In race games the same list of words is duplicated for each player. The round winner is the player who caught all his words the fastest, or the most words before the round expires.
Rounds

The number of winning rounds.

Language

The language of the generated words.

Words count

In race games, this is the number of words per round. In capture games, the number of words per round is that number multiplied by the number of players.

Words length

The words length presets:

  • Shortest is 3 to 5 letters
  • shorter is 4 to 6 letters
  • standard is 5 to 8 letters
  • longer is 6 to 10 letters
  • longest is 7 to 12 letters

Game play

When typing the words, the case does not matter, you can use write lowercase.

Using the Space bar clears the input.

Game rounds have an expiration, i.e. after a while the remaining words are not available anymore and the scores are accessible.

Scores

At the end of a round, the round scores and game scores are displayed.

The round scores show the number of caught words and the type speed in words per minute.

The game scores show the number of rounds won and the best type speed so far.

The scores also show some awards:

  • First for the player who caught the first word of the round
  • Longest for the player who first caught the longest word
  • Double for any player who caught two words in a row, only in capture mode
  • Last for the player who caught the last word of the round, only in capture mode
