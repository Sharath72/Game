Game creation

Mode

The game mode.

In capture games the players compete for the words, the first player that typed a word wins it. The round winner is the player who caught the most words before the round expires.

In race games the same list of words is duplicated for each player. The round winner is the player who caught all his words the fastest, or the most words before the round expires.

Rounds

The number of winning rounds.

Language

The language of the generated words.

Words effect

An optional effect used to display the words.

None: no effect, the word is displayed as is, e.g.

Hidden: one letter is hidden for words of up to 6 letters, two letters between 7 and 10 letters, three letters otherwise, e.g.

Reverse: the letters are displayed in reverse order, e.g.

Anagram: the letters are shuffled, e.g.

Words count

In race games, this is the number of words per round. In capture games, the number of words per round is that number multiplied by the number of players.

Words length

The words length presets. Shortest is 3 to 5 letters, shorter is 4 to 6, standard is 5 to 8, longer is 6 to 10, longest is 7 to 12.
